About Us

Bharat C Enterprises was founded in 1979 by Subhash C. Basra, a visionary entrepreneur who built the company from the ground up with a strong emphasis on integrity, quality, and dependable service. Under his leadership, the company established itself as a trusted chemical trading partner for manufacturers across India.

In 2004–2005, his son Vinay Basra, took over the reins. With a forward-looking approach and deep respect for the company’s core values, he introduced modern business practices, expanded operations, and strengthened the company's presence across key sectors.

Under his leadership, Bharat C Enterprises has grown, serving major industries such as cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, cleaning agents, and textiles. The company is known for its quality-assured products, prompt service, and long-standing client relationships.

Today, Bharat C continues to evolve while staying rooted in the legacy of excellence that has defined it for over four decades.
